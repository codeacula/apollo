# Summary

Collect the adoption, feedback, research, and delight work that shapes Apollo's personality and onboarding experience over time.

## Goals

- improve onboarding and feedback loops for new users
- gather supporting product/context research that may influence prompting and product behavior
- preserve room for delight-oriented features without mixing them into core reliability work

## Exit Criteria

- Apollo has a clearer path for onboarding and collecting user feedback
- contextual research is captured in a way that can inform product decisions responsibly
- delight-oriented features are organized without distracting from core roadmap work

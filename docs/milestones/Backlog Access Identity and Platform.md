# Summary

Organize the access-control, identity, and platform-expansion work that broadens how Apollo can be installed, managed, and used across people and environments.

## Goals

- support more explicit access request and approval flows
- model identity and platform relationships more flexibly across servers and clients
- reduce dependence on one delivery channel while preserving security and manageability

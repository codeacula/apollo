# Summary

Capture the reminder and to-do quality improvements that matter for everyday usability but do not need to block the current short-term reminder roadmap.

## Goals

- improve to-do and reminder ergonomics after the core reminder foundation is in place
- clean up reminder and daily-plan edge cases that reduce trust in the product
- refine reminder and to-do interaction flows without mixing them into the core milestone path

## Exit Criteria

- reminder and to-do flows feel cleaner and less error-prone in regular use
- obvious quality-of-life gaps in daily reports and quick-create flows are addressed
- reminder workflow design debt is reduced enough to support future product polish work
